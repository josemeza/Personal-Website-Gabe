José Meza,
Art Director
About
José Meza is an art director based in San Francisco currently working on visual and brand design with Google Brand Studio. Previously, he worked at IDEO, helped launch Figma, and interned at Pentagram and 2x4. He studied architecture at Yale and Princeton and makes architectural images and objects and shares them on Instagram. Thanks for stopping by.
